Portfolio #1

In mid-2018 I created one of the IoT projects, which is a device to provide automatic notification of remaining gallon water or LPG gas, which has a real-time board and recording data for every gallon replacement or LPG gas. The stages that I do start from designing, unifying components, programming until ready-to-use. The main components that I use include NodeMCU as a data processor and Load Cell as a weight detector. I also use free IoT platforms such as Thinger.io to display real-time dashboards and the IFTTT platform to provide notifications to users and distributors of gallons and LPG gas.
